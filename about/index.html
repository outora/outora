About us

Outora Orbital Technologies (Outora) is an Australian space company.

Our goal is to help build a future among the stars by engaging with and enabling passionate people to gain hands on experience with space technologies.

We are currently working to achieve this goal with our orbital space program, which allows anyone to get involved with a real life orbital space mission.

We must invest our efforts into developing the skills and technologies to help us expand and master the galaxy around us. This is our greatest challenge for the next few hundred years.
